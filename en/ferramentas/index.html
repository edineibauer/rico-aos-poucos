Frequently Asked Questions

Questions and Answers

Are the tools really free?
Yes, 100%. All Rico aos Poucos tools are completely free, with no registration required, no paid plans and no invasive advertising. Our goal is to democratize access to financial planning tools.
Is my data saved? Is it safe?
Your data is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). We don't send any information to external servers. Your privacy is completely respected. If you clear your browser data, the information will be deleted.
Can I use it on mobile?
Yes! All tools are responsive and work perfectly on smartphones, tablets and computers. You can even install them as an app (PWA) on your phone's home screen for quick access.
Are the calculations accurate?
The tools use standard financial formulas and reliable reference data (such as CUB/SINAPI for construction). They are excellent for planning and estimates, but remember they are simulations - for important decisions, consult specialized professionals.
Will you add more tools?
Yes! We are constantly developing new tools and improving existing ones. If you have suggestions for calculators you'd like to see, contact us.
