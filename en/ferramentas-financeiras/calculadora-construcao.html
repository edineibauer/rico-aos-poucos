Construction Cost Calculator

Estimate how much it costs to build your house based on updated SINAPI data and market research.

About this calculator

Data source

Values are based on SINAPI (National System for Research on Costs and Indices of Civil Construction) from IBGE, updated with Nov/2025 data, supplemented with market research for specific items.

How to use

  • Configure the construction type, finishing standard and your region
  • Adjust specific materials (windows, floors, roof, etc.)
  • Uncheck professionals whose cost you won't have (e.g., DIY)
  • Add extras like pool, BBQ area, wall
  • Results update automatically as you adjust options
