FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build a house in Brazil in 2026?
The average cost ranges from R$ 1,500 to R$ 3,500 per sqm, depending on the finish standard, region and construction method. A 100sqm medium-standard house costs between R$ 180,000 and R$ 280,000, not including land.
Which is cheaper: masonry or EPS?
EPS is usually 5-10% cheaper than conventional masonry. Savings come from lighter foundation (lighter structure = cheaper foundation), less material waste and reduced construction time (6-8 months vs 10-14 months).
What is included in the cost per sqm?
Includes: foundation, structure, walls, roofing, electrical and plumbing installations, basic finishes (floors, painting, fixtures). Not included: land, architectural projects, city hall fees, furniture and external items like pool and wall.
Does the calculator consider my region?
Yes! The calculator automatically adjusts costs based on the CUB (Basic Unit Cost) of each Brazilian state. Regions like Sao Paulo, Rio de Janeiro and Santa Catarina have higher costs than the national average.
Can I export the budget?
Yes! You can export the complete budget to Excel with all details of materials, labor, extras and projects. Ideal to present to builders or use as a negotiation base.
