Frequently Asked Questions

Questions and Answers

What is the sector simulator?
It's an educational tool that allows you to analyze the historical performance of different investment sectors (fixed income, stocks, REITs, dollar, etc.) and simulate how diversified portfolios would have performed in different periods.
How does the simulation work?
You define a percentage allocation across available sectors and choose a historical period. The simulator shows how that portfolio would have behaved, allowing you to compare different diversification strategies.
Which sectors can I analyze?
The simulator includes major sectors: Fixed Income (CDI, Inflation+), Brazilian Stocks (IBOV), REITs, Dollar, Gold, S&P 500, Bitcoin and others. Each sector represents an asset class with its own characteristics.
Is the data reliable?
The data is based on public indices and official sources. We use references like CDI, IPCA, IBOV, S&P 500, historical dollar and gold prices. The tool is for educational and analytical purposes.
Is this investment advice?
No. This is an educational tool to help you understand the historical behavior of sectors. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Consult a professional before investing.
