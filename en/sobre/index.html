About Rico aos Poucos

The Philosophy

"Rico aos Poucos" (Rich Little by Little) is not just a name, it's a life and investment philosophy. I believe that true wealth is built with patience, discipline, and knowledge - not with promises of quick gains or magic formulas.

This project was born from the desire to share views, analyses, and learnings about the financial market in an accessible and honest way. Here you won't find "hot tips" or unrealistic promises - just well-founded analyses and transparent opinions.

The Purpose

My goal with this space is to help people:

  • Understand the macroeconomic scenario and how it affects your investments
  • Make more informed decisions about asset allocation
  • Develop a long-term mindset for wealth building
  • Avoid common mistakes that harm beginner investors
